How to play
Provide your responses to the questions posed by the system so that you can retrieve the data successfully! Use the two hint supports if required!

Do not get detected and finish the task on time!
Controls: Use simple mouse clicks on interactive and non-interactive items.
Be Aware of:
Cameras - don't get into their vision!
Guards - If they see you, you die!
Laser beams - You will turn into ashes if you touch them!
G7 FRT - The auto mounted turret gun will shoot you if you get detected!
Don't let the guard spot you. Hide behind the purple door!
Get the job done, and you get promoted with a trophy! Good luck Code 57.
